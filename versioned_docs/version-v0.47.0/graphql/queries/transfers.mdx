get a list of all transfers for a pubkey

```graphql
transfers(
  pubkey: String!
  isFrom: Boolean
  isTo: Boolean
): [Transfer!]
```


### Arguments

#### `pubkey` ([`String`](/docs/v0.47/graphql/scalars/string))

the pubkey to look for


#### `isFrom` ([`Boolean`](/docs/v0.47/graphql/scalars/boolean))

is the pubkey on the sending part of the transfer


#### `isTo` ([`Boolean`](/docs/v0.47/graphql/scalars/boolean))

is the pubkey in the receiving part of the transfer


### Type

#### [`Transfer`](/docs/v0.47/graphql/objects/transfer) 

A user initiated transfer
